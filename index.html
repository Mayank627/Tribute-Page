Ash Ketchum

One of the greatest trainers in the Pokemon World.


Here are his greatest achievements:

  • Top 16 in his first pokemon leauge
  • Defeated arch-rival Gary Oak in the Johto League
  • Orange League Champion
  • Battle Frontier Champion
  • Beat prime nemesis Paul in the Sinnoh Conference
  • Reached Kalos League finals
  • Trained Greninja to battle-bond phenomenon
  • Alola leaguechampion
  • Beat legendaries like Darkrai and Articuno
  • Beat the gyms of 7 regions
  • Currently in the Ultra Class of World Coronation Series
"Ash Ketchum battles with his heart. He's the bravest of all trainers."

--Professor Oak
